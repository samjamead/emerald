As well as Coaching, I’ve been designing and delivering Training since 2012. My training focuses on helping people have better conversations. Coaching plays an important part in my training too as it doesn’t have to be a one-to-one experience.

Coaching

The Coaching could be focused on issues and challenges in your personal life.

Or it could be about a work issue affecting you or one of your colleagues. In a work context, I’m experienced working at all levels, from new starters through to Executive Coaching at Board level.

Either way, it’s made up of a series of one to one sessions, ideally face to face and typically around 90 minutes long. Usually, we would agree the specific topic you want to discuss in these sessions in advance and we would always have an initial chat first before you commit to any formal arrangements, just to see how we get on!
